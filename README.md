# Sports Search Prototype

A next-generation sports search platform designed to empower super fans with comprehensive, real-time information about their favorite teams, players, and sports.

## Overview

Sports Search is a prototype platform built to serve the information needs of passionate sports enthusiasts. Whether you're looking for live scores, historical statistics, player analytics, or team insights, this tool aims to be your one-stop destination for all things sports.

## Problems

When a sports fan searches today, here's what typically happens:
"Lakers score" or "Lakers vs Celtics" leads to Google's sports widget showing the score, basic game stats, and a play-by-play text feed. It's functional but sterile - just facts and numbers. For live games, you get updates but they're delayed and lack the energy of what's actually happening.
"LeBron James" brings up his stats page, recent news articles from ESPN or Bleacher Report, and maybe some YouTube highlights in a carousel. But the news is often stale - published hours ago - and you have to click through to multiple sites to get the full picture.
"LeBron James dunk tonight" might surface some YouTube clips, but they're not organized by recency during a live game. You're hoping the algorithm caught the right moment, and often you end up on X/Twitter because that's where the clip spread first.
The problem is clear: the live emotional experience lives on social media or X, the highlights live on YouTube, the stats live on ESPN or Google, and the tribal community discussion is fragmented across Reddit, X, and Discord. 

We need to combine these for the ultimate sports experience.
