A next-generation sports search platform designed to empower super fans with comprehensive, real-time information about their favorite teams, players, and sports.

## Overview

Sports Search is a prototype platform built to serve the information needs of passionate sports enthusiasts. Whether you're looking for live scores, historical statistics, player analytics, or team insights, this tool aims to be your one-stop destination for all things sports.
